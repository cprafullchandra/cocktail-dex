About Cocktails

  • What does this app do?
    CocktailDex aims to make the lives of drinking newcomers and efficionados easier! We hope to do so by creating a community where all can find inspiration for new drinks, whether based on your pantry or your desire to try something new.
  • How can I find new drinks quickly?
    Easy! You can either search by ingredient/name, or if you're looking to create a drink from specific ingredients, you can add them to your Shelf and Search your Shelf!
  • What are the different Glass Types about?
    Traditionally, certain drinks are served in different glasses. High ball glasses are reserved for drinks expected to contain 10 to 14 oz. of fluid. They generally involve drinks mixed with a lot of juice, mixer, or soda. Lowball drinks are used for cocktails involving 3 or 4 oz of juice, alongside 1.5 oz of spirits. And of course the martini glass is yet another. We leave the cocktail glass as a generic glass that you may specify if you are ever unsure!
